Parting Emerald Seas With The Crimson Moon

The crimson moon carries the tides of emotion to emerald waters. Watch the actions of people who live by the emerald seas and under the crimson moon. Feel the emotions of children who feel alone. See how some deal with the abuse of a loved one. Delve deep into the mind of someone who regrets an unforgivable decision. Will they be forgiven, or will they not get the chance to be? Learn how depression affects a community and isolates a person. Teetering between happiness and the endless, some look to the crimson moon for solutions. Others look to the emerald sea for a calm resting spot. How would one handle the death of one they held so dear? Is it right to look to the moon and sea to fix a problem?
